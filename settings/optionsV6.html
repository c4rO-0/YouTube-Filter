Keyword example:

keyword1, keyword2, keyword3; channel name

Rules:

  • Search results will match all keywords
  • Every entry should have at least one keyword
  • Every entry should have channel name, except it is a playlist
  • Separate multiple keywords with comma(,)
  • Separate keywords and channel name with colon(;)
  • Prefix comma and colon (,;) with backslash(\), if your keywords contain them i.e. key\,word1 NOT key,word1.

Tips

  • Double click on keywords can toggle editing
  • Drag to sort
  • If you find your entry not worked, use YouTube search to correct your entry
  • In order to Import your Playlist, please stay logged in YouTube
  • For performance considerations, up to 10 entries are allowed, the excesses will be turned off

添加关键词例子:

关键词1, 关键词2, 关键词3;频道名称

规则

  • 关键词使用逗号(,)分隔, 注意是英文逗号(半角)
  • 关键词和频道使用分号(;)分隔, 注意使用英文分号(半角)
  • 搜索结果会匹配所有关键词,至少要有一个关键词
  • 最好可以添加频道名字,可以匹配的更精准
  • 如果需要逗号或分号 (,;) 请使用反斜线(\), 比如`关键\,词1`而不是`关键,词1`

贴士

  • 双击已存在关键词可以再次编辑
  • 点住关键词可以进行拖拽
  • 如果搜索结果不理想，可以去Youtube搜索栏中手动检验该关键词
  • 导入YouTube的播放列表,需要您的YouTube处于登录状态
  • 为了保证插件的顺利运行,目前只允许最多10个关键词条,多余的将会被自动关闭
